Privacy Policy & License

TL;DR

We do not collect any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device. Use the software at your own risk.

1. Privacy Commitment

CopyThat is built by Open Form LLC with a simple philosophy: your data is yours. The application runs entirely locally on your machine. We do not transmit, store, or sell any of the 2FA codes it detects. There is no tracking, no telemetry, and no analytics collection of any kind.

2. Data Handling

CopyThat parses incoming SMS messages for verification codes using a local detection engine. All processing happens in real-time, in memory, and data is not persisted beyond its functional use. No information is ever uploaded to any cloud service.
